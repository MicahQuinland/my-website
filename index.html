About me

Hey! My name is Micah. I love to learn new things, I love technology, and I enjoy mixing the two as often as possible. This website is one product of that. I won’t bore you with my life story and where I got my engineering degrees from, all of that is in my LinkedIn and Resume both linked somewhere on this site. However, I will say Go Cards!

Professionally I am an aspiring technical manager. I want to specialize in cloud infrastructure and services. I like to help people, solve problems, and do more than just know how to code. I am a huge fan of Shark Tank and have hopes to eventually start my own business, perhaps, starting with consulting.

I could go on and on about how I made this website, how it’s 100% hosted on Amazon Web Services, the automated deployment pipeline, or the messaging service, but I won’t. Enjoy my website and contact me if you wish!
